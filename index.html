Welcome to Mega-Pol Taxes & Accounting, Inc.

Mega-Pol Taxes & Accounting Inc., offers accounting and tax services to individuals and businesses.

The high professional standard of the services rendered by our company is guaranteed by the President of our company Arek Wyrzykowski who received Master Degree in Accountancy from University of Phoenix. Also he passed exam in IRS and became Registered Tax Return Preparer (RTRP). He is also a former teacher in accounting at the Academy of Business and Career Development in Chicago.

We provide services to our clients in English and Polish, we also offer telephone and internet advice and consulting.

Our clients include both the entities which have been in operation for some time as well as newly established businesses whom we are ready to assist to take the first steps on the market and to go through all necessary formalities.

Our company is licensed to provide notary services, thanks to which any agreements and all other documents which require to be notarized can be prepared and signed quickly and efficiently.
